Shapping

shAPPing is a mobile shopping application with the purpose of making grocery shopping more simple, convenient and efficient. The application in itself, paired with a smartphone, replaces modern self-scanning devices and systems often seen in stores today. It makes use of the smartphones bluetooth functionality along with unique electronic price tags throughout the stores, acting as bluetooth beacons - effectively removing the need of a separate hand-held device.

Technologies: HTML+CSS, Javascript, Bootstrap, Onsen UI, Apache Cordova, Firebase

FlightViz

FlightViz, previously Flightminder, is an interactive tool for visualizing CO2-emissions derived from business travel by flight, made by employees at the Royal Institute of Technology (KTH). The tool is created by a group of students in collaboration with a research group, FLIGHT, whose research is focused on decreasing CO2-emissions in flight-intensive organizations at KTH.
